Zakynthos, Greece


Postcard-perfect Navagio beach on Zakynthos Island is one of the most photographed spots and has been named the prettiest beach in Greece due to its infamous shipwreck resting on its shores.

Framed by limestone cliffs; it is also a world favourite for base jumpers, so adventurists can enjoy the best views of the crystal clear water.

Tourists Attractions

  1. Zákynthos Town (Zante Town)
  2. Byzantine Museum
  3. Roma Mansion
  4. Shipwreck beach (Navagio beach)
  5. Keri Caves
  6. Blue Caves

Cancun, Mexico


Not just for American spring breakers, Cancun plays host to a range of exciting activities sure to keep the whole family busy.

Named Mexico’s number one family-friendly holiday destination by TripAdvisor, visitors can snorkel through the world’s largest underwater sculpture museum, visit adventure parks, rest in the sunshine on one of Cancun’s many lively beaches or venture a little further and take a day trip to see the ancient Maya Ruins.

Things to do

  1. Cancun Wildlife
  2. Remote Villages near Cancun
  3. Salsa Dancing Lessons in Cancun
  4. Mexican Cooking Classes
  5. Cancun’s Nightlife
  6. Cancun Water Activities

Menorca, Spain


A shy destination among Spain’s party-loving Balearic Islands, Menorca offers one of the world’s most spectacular rooftop bars.

Indulge in a delicious cocktail whilst enjoying the Mediterranean sunset at Cova d’en Xoroi, move over Café Mambo Ibiza.

  2. Enjoy a view from the top – Go Paragliding
  3. Explore Menorca on horseback
  4. Experience the ultimate sundowner
  5. Swim to an isolated beach

Dubrovnik, Croatia


Fronting the Adriatic Sea, Dubrovnik is a charming coastal city with a distinctive history. Best known for its Old Town and more recently as the backdrop for Game of Thrones, visitors can tread in character’s footsteps and bring the fantasy to life.

Not forgetting Dubrovnik’s impressive medieval stone walls have been voted the top thing to do when visiting the ancient city- it’s 7not one to miss.
